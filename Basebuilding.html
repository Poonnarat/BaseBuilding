  • Villagers will have skill caps based on genetics?

  • Politics within a kingdom

  • Requests from the king, wartime etc
  • Politics within multiple kingdoms if possible
  • Fulfill villager needs
  • Food, housing, quality of life
  • Entertainment etc
  • Refining resources and producing goods
  • Iron ore to iron to armour/weapons etc
  • Technology progression
